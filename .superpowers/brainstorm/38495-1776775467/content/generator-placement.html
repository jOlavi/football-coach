Ryhmägeneraattori – missä se näkyy?

Where should the group generator live inside the training session builder?

A

Oma osio harjoituksen lopussa

Session builder has a dedicated "Groups & Teams" section at the bottom, separate from exercises. You can generate groups multiple times (e.g. once for drills, once for the final game).

B

Inline harjoitteen sisällä

Each exercise can optionally have a group assignment attached to it. Generate groups per exercise. More flexible but more complex UI.

C

Erillinen välilehti / paneeli

Inside the session builder, a second tab "Ryhmät" where all group generations live. Keeps the exercise list clean.
